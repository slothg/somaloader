Soundstep - SomaLoader

SomaLoader works slightly differently depending if you deploy your movie for Flash Player 9 or 10.

For a Flash Player version 9, the SomaLoader cache won't be automatically used and you will have to register the fonts loaded manually (Font.registerFont(MyFontClass)), this is automatically done if you deploy it for a Flash Player Version 10.
